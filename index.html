Twilight Tranquility


Stanza 1

As sunset falls upon the sea
Golden hues dance with glee
Peaceful waves caress the shore
Serenity forever more

Stanza 2

The stars appear in evening's sky
Twinkling diamonds, passing by
Night's soft breeze whispers secrets low
In the darkness, dreams begin to grow


Stanza 3

In this tranquil and peaceful place
I find solace, a gentle space
Where worries fade and love shines bright
Filling my heart with delight
